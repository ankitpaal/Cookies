OUR EXPERIANCE


A Company with solid experience BCA COOKIES operates in the baking industry since 2018, producing and selling biscuits, cookies and other pastry products.

We have 2 production facilities in India with 19 production lines, an installed capacity of 55 thousand tons, and we export to 30 city (75% of our production). BCA COOKIES founded “Family Cookies Category” in India, maintaining the leadership until today.

We are the second largest “Bca Butter Cookies” producer in India and the leading “cookies” producer in the India .
